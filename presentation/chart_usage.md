# Chart Usage Guide

Every chart in `outputs/figures` is intentionally tied to one or more slides.

| Figure | Best Slides | What the Speaker Should Say |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `closing_price_trend.png` | 4, 9, 10 | Compares broad price movement across selected tickers and sets up the trend story. |
| `moving_average_trend.png` | 6, 10, 12 | Moving averages smooth daily noise; short-term lines react faster than long-term lines. |
| `daily_returns_distribution.png` | 9, 10, 13 | Daily returns usually cluster near zero, but outliers show why caution is needed. |
| `volume_trend.png` | 8, 9, 13 | Volume spikes show periods of unusually high market participation. |
| `correlation_heatmap.png` | 10, 12 | Correlation explains whether selected stocks historically moved together. |
| `probability_up_down_by_ticker.png` | 10, 12 | Up/down frequency is descriptive historical evidence, not forecast confidence. |
